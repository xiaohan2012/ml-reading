\section{Conclusion}
\label{sec:conclusion}


This work introduces \relbench, a benchmark to facilitate research on relational deep learning~\citep{fey2023relational}. 
\relbench provides diverse and realistic relational databases and define practical predictive tasks that cover both entity-level prediction and entity link prediction.
In addition, we provide the first open-source implementation of relational deep learning and validated its effectiveness over the common practice of manual feature engineering by an experienced data scientist.
We hope \relbench will catalyze further research on relational deep learning to achieve highly-accurate prediction over complex multi-tabular datasets without manual feature engineering.
